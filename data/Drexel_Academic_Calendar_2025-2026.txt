DREXEL UNIVERSITY — QUARTER ACADEMIC CALENDAR 2025–2026
==========================================================================

ABOUT THE ACADEMIC YEAR
The majority of Drexel academic programs operate on the University's quarter system.
The academic year is based on four three-month terms:
- Fall term:   Late September to mid-December
- Winter term: Early January to end of March
- Spring term: Early April to mid-June
- Summer term: Late June to mid-September

The regular weekly schedule of classes includes Saturday sessions.

NOTE: Drexel is transitioning to a semester calendar for all programs on August 23, 2027.
