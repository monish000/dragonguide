PREREQUISITES, RESTRICTIONS, AND OVERRIDES
==========================================================================

COURSE PREREQUISITES
Courses may require prior coursework, minimum GPA, class standing, or major/college
restrictions. Prerequisites are listed in the Term Master Schedule (TMS) and course catalog.

REGISTRATION ERRORS — COMMON TYPES
- Prerequisite/Test Score Error: Student does not meet published prerequisite
- Class Restriction: Course limited by college, major, level, or permission
- Closed Section: No seats available
- Time Conflict: Overlapping meeting times
- Credit Limit: Exceeds maximum credits allowed for the term
- Hold on Record: Administrative hold blocks registration

PREREQUISITE OVERRIDE
If a student has equivalent preparation, they may request a prerequisite override from:
1. The academic department offering the course, OR
2. Their academic advisor (who coordinates with the department)

Overrides are not automatic; departments evaluate each request.

CLOSED SECTION OVERRIDE
- Last day to secure a Closed Section Override is published on the academic calendar
  (typically Friday of Week 1 for Full Term / Part of Term A)
- Requires department permission and advisor processing

PERMISSION / SPECIAL AUTHORIZATION
Some courses require department permission numbers entered during registration in DrexelOne.

VERIFY BEFORE REGISTERING
- Check TMS (drexel.edu/webtms) for restrictions and seat availability
- Schedule Ahead does NOT enforce all restrictions — verify separately
