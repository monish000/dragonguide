GRADUATE STUDENT REGISTRATION — KEY GUIDANCE
==========================================================================

ADVISOR CONSULTATION
Graduate students should consult the Academic Calendars on the Provost website frequently
for all academic dates and deadlines. Consult your college, school, and assigned graduate
academic advisor(s) for program-specific dates and requirements.

REGISTRATION VIA DREXELONE
- Log in to DrexelOne > Academics tab > Register for Course
- Review Registration Time Ticket before registering
- Agree to Student Financial Obligation (SFO) Statement each term
- Confirm enrollment each term after registering

TIME TICKETS
Registration time tickets assign the date and time a student may begin registering.
Students can view time ticket information in DrexelOne prior to the registration period.

SCHEDULE AHEAD
- Available from Week 4 of the prior term through Week 1 of the upcoming term
- Create up to 10 sample schedules before the time ticket opens
- Does NOT reserve seats; does NOT bypass registration holds
- Does NOT validate all course restrictions — verify on Term Master Schedule (TMS)

MASTER'S STUDENTS — TYPICAL REQUIREMENTS (varies by program)
- Most master's programs require 45–48 quarter credits
- Plan of study maintained in DegreeWorks (DrexelOne > Academics)
- Continuous enrollment required unless on approved Leave of Absence
- Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) applies to graduate students receiving federal aid
- Thesis-option and dissertation students have additional defense and submission deadlines

APPLICATION FOR DEGREE
- Submit Application for Degree in DrexelOne by the term deadline (see academic calendar)
- Doctoral candidates and thesis-option master's students: check Graduate College graduation
  pages for defense notification deadlines (typically two weeks before defense)
