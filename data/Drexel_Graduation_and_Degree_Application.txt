HOW TO APPLY FOR YOUR DEGREE — STEP BY STEP
==========================================================================

STEP 1: Access Your Degree Application
1. Log in to DrexelOne
2. Select the "Academics" tab
3. Select "Apply for Graduation" in the "Grades, Transcripts, and Records" channel

STEP 2: Select Your Most Recent Program Term
- Choose the term you were LAST ENROLLED in for the program you intend to graduate from.
- Do NOT automatically select the current term — verify this is correct.

STEP 3: Verify Your Program Details
- Your current active program(s) will appear.
- If you have two active programs (e.g., BSIS-IST and BSBA-COBA), apply for EACH separately.
- If program information is incorrect, contact your academic advisor to update before applying.

STEP 4: Identify Your Anticipated Term of Graduation
- Select the term/year when you plan to complete ALL degree requirements.

STEP 5: Verify the Name on Your Diploma
- Default is your legal/birth name as currently in the system.
- You may select "Current Name" or "Preferred Name" from the dropdown.
- To display a full middle name (instead of initial), select "New" and edit.
- If your official name is incorrect, follow the Updating Personal Information process first.

STEP 6: Indicate the Mailing Address for Your Diploma
- Diploma will be mailed to the address you provide.
- Choose from saved addresses in DrexelOne or enter a new one.

STEP 7: Review and Submit
- Review the Degree Application Summary carefully.
- Select "Continue" to submit.
- The Degree Application Confirmation page confirms successful submission.

==========================================================================
VIEWING YOUR SUBMITTED DEGREE APPLICATION
==========================================================================
1. Go to DrexelOne > Academics tab
2. Select "More BannerWeb Student Services" in the "Grades, Transcripts, and Records" channel
3. Select "Student Records"
4. Select "View Your Application(s) for Degree"

==========================================================================
CHANGING YOUR DIPLOMA MAILING ADDRESS
==========================================================================
1. Log in to DrexelOne
2. Select "Account" in the upper right corner
3. Select "Personal Information"
4. In the Addresses section, select "Edit" for "Diploma Address"
